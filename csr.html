Corporate Social Responsibility

LAW PARK EDUCATIONAL TRUST
We Charulatha.M.R, Partner and S.M.Manjunatha, Managing Partner of LAW PARK ASSOCIATES, Bangalore, India are professional practising advocates, formed and running this law firm by name, LAW PARK ASSOCIATES.

We have been observing from our educational times onwards, that there are a large number of our contemporary students in our society are lacking sufficient resources and encouragement to pursue and complete quality education and later on due to this deficiency, get settled in a sub-standard position in their life, even though they possess enough aptitude and brilliance in education. It is a loss to both individuals and the nation in respect of quality citizenship. We have with the intention to subside this problem to the extent possible by us, pondered together and started an educational trust by name LAW PARK EDUCATIONAL TRUST (‘LPET’) and help present such students to get an affordability of quality education through LPET, so that their better future life is not put out.
LPET is a non-profitable organisation run by us, we, Mrs. Charulatha M.R. as Managing Trustee and Mr. S.M. Manjunatha as the other Trustee. We give sponsorship to deserving children from any Standard upto 10th Standard for the time being, selected every academic year and encourage them to complete their secondary education with quality at school level, irrespective of their gender, religion, caste, place within India, etc. With this aspect borne in mind, LPET takes the responsibility to pay the annual school fees for such deserving number of children confined to only with the available funds without any borrowings.
To reasonably achieve the objects of our social responsibility, apart from our own contribution, we also get financial support from our relatives, friends, clients and well wishers in India and abroad.
All you have to do to be a part of this noble cause is by contributing generously
